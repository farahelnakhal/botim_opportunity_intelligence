# VE-003 — Hold-Underwriting Demand Test

- **Experiment ID:** VE-003
- **Proposition tested:** OPP-010 — Settlement assurance + hold underwriting (`../product-ideas/opp-010-settlement-assurance-hold-underwriting.md`)
- **Hypothesis:** ≥30% of contacted UAE online SME merchants who experienced a settlement hold or freeze of >14 days in the last 24 months will complete a waitlist for an acceptance product with guaranteed T+1 settlement where risk holds become priced advances (fee stated up front as 1–2% of the held amount).
- **Target participants:** owners of online SMEs in `SEG-uae-online-sme-psp-merchants` and `SEG-uae-marketplace-sellers` with a self-reported hold/freeze event >14 days.
- **Recruitment criteria:**
  - Include: active trade licence; online acceptance or marketplace disbursement as primary revenue channel; hold event verifiable in outline (provider, duration, amount band).
  - Exclude: merchants in scheme-prohibited or clearly high-risk MCCs (their exclusion is itself data — count them separately); team contacts; anyone recruited via BOTIM promotion.
- **Method:** two stages. Stage 1 — 12 interviews (past behaviour first: what happened, what it cost, what they did next, whether they left the provider). Stage 2 — waitlist offer to ≥40 qualified merchants; the form states the pricing principle (holds become advances at a stated fee) and requires trade-licence number and current PSP. Non-leading rules per `opportunity-intelligence/templates/validation-experiment.md`.
- **Sample size:** 12 interviews + ≥40 waitlist offers — enough to separate 30% success from 10% failure at kill-test precision; also counts the high-risk-MCC exclusion rate (disconfirmation data for the pre-mortem).
- **Success threshold (pre-committed):** ≥30% waitlist completion AND ≥7 of 12 interviewees describe the hold as a switching trigger unprompted.
- **Failure threshold (pre-committed):** ≤10% waitlist completion, OR ≥7 of 12 interviewees operate in categories our own risk policy would hold (the wedge is a niche).
- **Duration:** 3 weeks; hard stop.
- **Data collected:** hold event details (provider, duration, amount band), verbatims (tagged for Workstream A hand-back via the evidence-request queue), waitlist completion, current PSP, MCC-exclusion count. Stored here; personal data minimised.
- **Decision informed:** whether OPP-010 proceeds to concierge advance-against-receivables pilot design plus the regulatory review, or is reclassified (Weak if the wedge is a high-risk niche). Decision owner: Workstream B via meeting-ready output.
