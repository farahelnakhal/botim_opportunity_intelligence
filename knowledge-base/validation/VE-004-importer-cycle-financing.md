# VE-004 — Importer Cycle-Financing Demand Test

- **Experiment ID:** VE-004
- **Proposition tested:** OPP-013 (`../product-ideas/opp-013-import-payment-working-capital.md`). Doubles as the upgrade instrument for `SEG-uae-importers-upfront-pay` (its Low confidence lifts only on first-person importer accounts — verbatims hand back to Workstream A).
- **Hypothesis:** ≥8 of 15 interviewed small UAE importers (below or outside the AED 1M fintech eligibility floor) confirm first-person the upfront-pay/collect-later working-capital gap, AND ≥25% of 40 offered accept a priced term sheet (≈1.5%/30 days, financing conditional on routing supplier payments through BOTIM).
- **Target participants:** owner-operators importing from China/India/Turkey via T/T, UAE mainland or free-zone, below/outside CredibleX-class eligibility.
- **Recruitment criteria:**
  - Include: active import licence or verifiable recent import shipments; supplier payments by wire/escrow; not currently financed by a bank LATR line.
  - Exclude: importers already using CredibleX/Comfi (count separately — their existence is competitor validation data); team contacts; BOTIM-promo recruits.
- **Method:** Stage 1 — 15 interviews, past behaviour first (last shipment: how paid, when collected, how the gap was funded, what that cost). Stage 2 — priced term-sheet offer to ≥40 qualified importers; pricing and the routing condition stated up front; acceptance = signed intent + trade-licence number.
- **Sample size:** 15 interviews (segment-confirmation saturation) + ≥40 offers (separates 25% success from 8% failure at kill-test precision).
- **Success threshold (pre-committed):** ≥8/15 first-person gap confirmations AND ≥25% term-sheet acceptance.
- **Failure threshold (pre-committed):** ≤4/15 gap confirmations (segment thesis fails — also fails Workstream A's upgrade condition), OR ≤8% term-sheet acceptance.
- **Duration:** 4 weeks; hard stop.
- **Data collected:** payment-timing map per importer (deposit %, transit days, collection days), gap-funding method and cost, term-sheet responses with objections verbatim, competitor-usage count. Verbatims tagged for Workstream A hand-back (REQ-007).
- **Decision informed:** OPP-013 proceeds to concierge MVP design (manual FX transfer + manual financing) or is reclassified Weak; SEG confidence upgrade decision belongs to Workstream A on the same data.
