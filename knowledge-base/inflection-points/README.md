# knowledge-base/inflection-points/ (Workstream A)

One record per market change, using `customer-intelligence/templates/inflection-point.md`. Files are named `IP-YYYY-nnn.md`, sequential within the year.

## What qualifies

A dated change in merchant behaviour, competitor landscape, infrastructure, regulation, or customer expectations that alters the **timing or importance** of an opportunity — e.g. a real-time-payments rollout, a competitor entering/withdrawing, a visible shift from cash to wallets in a segment, open-finance access changing what underwriting is possible.

A competitor shipping a routine feature is a change-log entry in their profile, **not** an inflection point. The bar: does it change what merchants do or expect, or when a window opens/closes?

## Rules

- Every record fills `Evidence against it` and `What would invalidate it` — an inflection point without a falsifier is an opinion.
- Status lifecycle: `emerging` → `confirmed` / `invalidated` → `played-out`. Invalidated records are kept — knowing what didn't happen is knowledge.
- Weekly updates re-check `emerging` records first.
