# Template — Customer Segment Profile

Never use "SMEs" as one segment. A segment is defined by shared behaviour, not just industry. File location: `knowledge-base/segments/SEG-<kebab-slug>.md`.

Good segment name: "Small UAE importers that pay suppliers upfront but collect from customers after 30–60 days." Bad: "UAE SMEs".
